Why Epicodus, Why Coding?

A few months ago I was exploring local event options around Portland on Facebook, looking for an activity to entertain family that was going to be in town. I came across a posting for Epicods' free Saturday Workshop, did a quick scan of the website, and was immediately intrgued. A month later I joined TechRise PDX and embarked on a new journey.

My decision to enroll in and attend the Epicodus coding bootcamp is one that adjusts my sails, but does not steer me completely off my original course. During my undergraduate education, I had set my sights on landing a role within either of the studios or big name corporations in town. As with any creative industry, competition is steep and the job market is buzzing with talent. Through my hunting I have decided that learning a new trade to enhance my value could be the edge I need and the niche I have been searching for. The need for people who understand code increases with every tech advancement and new app idea, and I see myself as a part of the fulfilment. Having taken a couple classes throughout my different levels of schooling, coding has always been something I have been interested in and have dabbled in, but was never able to fully experience or delve into. In the long run, a career involving code provides opportunity, growth, stability, security, and an upward trajectory. Code will allow me the ability to bring my creative ideas to life where I would have previously had to seek the help of someone else. I strongly feel that adding the element of coding to my photo and video skillset will make me the multi-faceted employee employers search for. I am excited for the possibilities Epicodus creates for my future.
